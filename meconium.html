Understanding Meconium-Stained Liquor

Risks Associated with Meconium-Stained Liquor

  • Risk of Meconium Aspiration Syndrome (MAS)
  • Potential for Fetal Distress
  • Increased Need for Neonatal Resuscitation
  • Possible Long-term Respiratory Complications

Typical Management Steps

  1. Continuous Fetal Monitoring
  2. Assessment of Meconium Thickness
  3. Preparation for Potential Neonatal Resuscitation
  4. Post-Delivery Newborn Assessment and Care
  5. Parental Education and Support
