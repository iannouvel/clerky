Clinical Guideline for the Use of Penthrox in Ambulatory Gynaecology
- Provides guidance on the use of Penthrox (methoxyflurane) for pain relief in adults
- Recommendations for offering Penthrox for various gynaecological procedures
- Contraindications and caution in use
- Side effects, warnings, and dosage information
- Staff training, prescribing, and administration guidelines
- Storage and disposal procedures
- Monitoring effectiveness through pain scores and procedure rates
- Appendices for version control, due regard assessment, and guidelines for implementation and monitoring.
