Fetal Blood Sampling (FBS) Maternity Protocol: MP038 Key Principles: Aims of FBS: To clarify fetal status in situations where the FHR trace is concerning. When FBS Should be Undertaken: Advised in the presence of abnormal FHR trace. Contraindications for FBS: Cervical dilatation of <3cm, maternal infection, fetal bleeding disorders, prematurity. Interpretation of FBS results: Normal, Borderline, Abnormal. Documentation: Must be documented on Badgernet. Paired Cord Blood Samples: Should be taken in cases of concern about the baby.
