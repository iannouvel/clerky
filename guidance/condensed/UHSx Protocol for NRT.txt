Protocol for the direct supply of nicotine replacement therapies (NRT) by registered midwives and tobacco dependency advisors in University Hospitals Sussex Foundation Trust. This document outlines the conditions for supplying NRT directly to pregnant patients over 12 years old who smoke and are receiving stop smoking support. NRT products are GSL medicines and can be supplied without a PGD or PSD. Tobacco dependency advisors are responsible for following the protocol and ensuring safe storage and handling of NRT products. Staff competencies include NCSCT level 2 training. NRT can be supplied to pregnant patients over 12 years old who smoke and are seeking to quit, with certain exclusions. Various forms of NRT can be supplied, with dosage and quantity instructions to be followed. Record-keeping is essential, including details of supply and patient information. Authorization must be renewed yearly.
