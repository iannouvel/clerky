This clinical guideline provides comprehensive advice on investigating and caring for small-for-gestational-age (SGA) and growth-restricted fetuses. Key recommendations include assessing risk factors, monitoring fetal growth with Doppler scans and ultrasound, and differentiating between SGA and FGR. Maternal factors and underlying causes play a significant role, emphasizing the need for surveillance and management strategies. Specific recommendations cover interventions, birth timing, and postnatal counseling. The guideline also addresses the impact of maternal lifestyle factors, medical conditions, and interventions on fetal growth. Evidence-based recommendations are provided, along with levels of evidence and grades of recommendation, to guide clinical decision-making.
