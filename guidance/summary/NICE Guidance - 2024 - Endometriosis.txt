Diagnosis and Presentation:
- Assess the extent of deep endometriosis involving the bowel, bladder, or ureter
- Interpret pelvic MRI scans by a healthcare professional with specialist expertise in gynaecological imaging
- Consider laparoscopy for diagnosis in women with suspected endometriosis, even if ultrasound was normal
- Consider pelvic ultrasound or MRI before operative laparoscopy for suspected deep endometriosis
- Perform systematic inspection of the pelvis during diagnostic laparoscopy by a gynaecologist with training in laparoscopic surgery
- Consider biopsy of suspected endometriosis during diagnostic laparoscopy
- Offer treatment based on symptoms, preferences, and priorities rather than stage of endometriosis

Treatment and Management:
- Discuss benefits and risks of analgesics for endometriosis-related pain
- Consider hormonal treatment for suspected, confirmed, or recurrent endometriosis
- Discuss surgical management options for suspected or confirmed endometriosis
- Perform laparoscopic surgery for endometriosis unless contraindications exist
- Consider excision over ablation for endometriomas
- Offer excision or ablation plus adhesiolysis for endometriosis not involving bowel, bladder, or ureter
- Offer laparoscopic ovarian cystectomy or drainage and ablation for endometriomas
- Discuss laparoscopic surgery benefits and risks for deep endometriosis in women trying to conceive
- Avoid hormonal treatment alone or with surgery for women trying to conceive

Monitoring Requirements:
- Monitor for outpatient follow-up, especially for deep endometriosis or large endometrioma
- Monitor for benefits and risks of analgesics
- Monitor for hormonal treatment effects
- Monitor post-surgical outcomes for endometriosis management

Contraindications and Cautions:
- Avoid hormonal treatment alone or in combination with surgery for women trying to conceive
- Caution against offering hormonal treatment for women trying to conceive
- Consider contraindications for laparoscopic surgery based on individual patient factors
