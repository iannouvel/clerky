### **Fetal Heart Monitoring Summary (MP037)**  

#### **Antenatal Monitoring**  
- **Purpose:** Detect fetal hypoxia/acidaemia.  
- **Low-risk pregnancies:** No electronic monitoring required.  
- **High-risk pregnancies:** Use CTG (preferably cCTG for objective analysis).  
- **STV (short-term variation):** Assess fetal heart fluctuations but not in isolation.  
- **Monitoring duration:** 60 minutes; escalate if criteria unmet.  

#### **Intrapartum Monitoring**  
- **Low-risk:** Intermittent auscultation (record FHR, palpate maternal pulse).  
- **High-risk:** Continuous CTG (1:1 care, regular reviews).  
- **Indications for continuous CTG:** Epidural, oxytocin, sepsis, maternal fever (>37.5Â°C), meconium, IUGR, previous cesarean, hypertension, diabetes, reduced fetal movements.  

#### **CTG Interpretation & Classification**  
- **Assess:** Baseline FHR, variability, accelerations, decelerations, contractions.  
- **Deceleration Types:**  
  - **White (Normal):** Early/variable decelerations without concerning features.  
  - **Amber (Suspicious):** Repetitive variables/late decelerations <30 min.  
  - **Red (Pathological):** Repetitive variables/late decelerations >30 min, acute bradycardia (>3 min).  
- **Accelerations:** Presence suggests fetal well-being; absence alone does not indicate acidosis.  

#### **Management Based on CTG Classification**  
- **Normal:** Continue hourly assessments.  
- **Suspicious:** Escalate to obstetrician, reposition mother, assess risk factors.  
- **Pathological:** Urgent obstetric review, exclude emergencies (cord prolapse, abruption), consider expediting birth.  
- **Bradycardia (>2 min):** Immediate obstetric review, prepare for urgent delivery.  

#### **Conservative Measures**  
- Reposition mother (avoid supine).  
- Treat hypotension (IV fluids if epidural-related).  
- Discontinue oxytocin if in use.  
- Exclude cord compression/acute events.  

#### **Key Points**  
- **STV:** Not interpretable before 60 minutes.  
- **Telemetry:** Use if mobility needed.  
- **Documentation:** Record all findings and interventions.  
- **Decision-making:** Discuss with birthing person, consider clinical context.  

**Note:** Expedite birth if persistent pathological CTG despite interventions.
