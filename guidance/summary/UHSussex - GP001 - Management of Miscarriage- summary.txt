The clinical guideline on the Management of Miscarriage provides key principles, responsibilities, and protocols for healthcare professionals involved in early pregnancy care. It covers the diagnosis and management of miscarriage, including conservative, medical, and surgical options. The guideline emphasizes the importance of providing information and support to patients, using sensitive language, and offering follow-up appointments. It also includes guidance on paperwork to be completed and the provision of anti-D rhesus prophylaxis. Additionally, it provides information on bereavement support services and resources for families who have experienced a miscarriage.
