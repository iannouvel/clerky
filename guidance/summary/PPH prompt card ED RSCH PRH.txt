### **PPH PROMPT CARD SUMMARY**  

**Emergency Response:**  
- Call **2222** for obstetric emergency (500-1000ml), major haemorrhage (1000-2000ml), or massive haemorrhage (>2000ml).  
- Immediate team response: obstetric team, labour ward coordinator, anaesthetist, and theatre team.  

**Initial Actions:**  
- Secure **2 x wide-bore IV cannulas**.  
- Take bloods: **FBC, clotting, U&Es, LFTs, ROTEM, crossmatch 4 units**.  
- Start **IV fluids** and **measure blood loss**.  

**Uterotonics (in order):**  
1. **Oxytocin 10IU IM** or **5IU IV bolus**.  
2. **Syntometrine 1ml IM** (avoid in hypertension).  
3. **Ergometrine 500mcg IM/IV** (max 2 doses, only 1 if Syntometrine given).  
4. **Oxytocin infusion** (40IU in 500ml NaCl at 125ml/hr).  
5. **Tranexamic acid 1g IV**.  
6. **Carboprost 250mcg IM** (max 8 doses, 15min apart).  
7. **Misoprostol 1000mcg PR** (consultant discussion).  

**Additional Measures:**  
- Inspect **placenta and perineum** for trauma.  
- Insert **urometer** for accurate output monitoring.  
- Consider **transfer to theatre** if bleeding persists.  

**Documentation & Reporting:**  
- Complete **PPH proforma**.  
- Submit **Datix** if blood loss >1500ml.  

*Focus on rapid intervention, uterotonics, and escalation to theatre if needed.*
