The clinical guideline on the Management of Gestational Trophoblastic Disease provides comprehensive information on the presentation, diagnosis, management, treatment, and follow-up of GTD and GTN. It covers topics such as the presentation of molar pregnancies, diagnosis methods, removal procedures, follow-up protocols, treatment options for GTN, and long-term outcomes. Recommendations are made for follow-up intervals, contraception, and support for women and their families. The guideline emphasizes the importance of individualized care, timely referrals, and close monitoring for optimal outcomes. Further research areas and useful links are provided for additional information and support.
