Executive summary Antenatal diagnosis and care of women with placenta praevia or a low-lying placenta What are the risk factors for women with placenta praevia or a low-lying placenta? Caesarean delivery is associated with an increased risk of placenta praevia in subsequent pregnancies. This risk rises as the number of prior caesarean sections increases.
