The clinical guideline for the Diagnosis and Management of Ectopic Pregnancy emphasizes the importance of transvaginal ultrasound as the primary diagnostic tool for various types of ectopic pregnancies. It provides detailed recommendations for the diagnosis and management of tubal, cervical, caesarean scar, interstitial, cornual, ovarian, abdominal, and heterotopic pregnancies. The guideline outlines specific criteria for each type of ectopic pregnancy and recommends appropriate treatment options, including expectant management, medical management with methotrexate, and surgical approaches. It also highlights the need for appropriate training for clinicians involved in the diagnosis and management of ectopic pregnancies and emphasizes the importance of providing support and counseling to women undergoing treatment.
