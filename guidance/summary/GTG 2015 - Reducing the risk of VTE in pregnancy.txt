This guideline provides evidence-based recommendations for gtg 2015 - reducing the risk of vte in pregnancy. It is intended for use by healthcare professionals in clinical practice. Executive summary of recommendations Prepregnancy and antenatal risk assessment What are the risk factors for venous thromboembolism (VTE) in pregnancy and the puerperium and what is the magnitude of risk for these factors? All women should undergo a documented assessment of risk factors for VTE in early pregnancy or prepregnancy.
