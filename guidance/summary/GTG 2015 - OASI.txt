Executive summary of recommendations Classification and terminology How should obstetric anal sphincter injury be classified? It is recommended that the classification outlined in this guideline be used when describing any obstetric anal sphincter injury. If there is any doubt about the degree of third-degree tear, it is advisable to classify it to the higher degree rather than the lower degree.
