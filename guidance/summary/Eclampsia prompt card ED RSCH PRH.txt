### **ECLAMPSIA PROMPT CARD**  

**Immediate Actions:**  
- Call **2222** for obstetric & neonatal emergency.  
- **ABCs**: Secure airway, give **O₂ 10L via face mask**, monitor BP and sats, insert **2 x grey cannulas**.  

**Seizure Management:**  
- **Magnesium Sulfate (MgSO₄)**:  
  - **Loading dose**: 4g IV over 5 mins (doctor-administered).  
  - **Maintenance**: 10g in 50ml at 5ml/hr via infusion pump.  
  - **Recurrent seizures**: Give **2g bolus IV over 3 mins**.  
  - **Monitor Mg levels**, keep **calcium gluconate** ready for toxicity.  

**BP Control:**  
- **IV Labetalol**:  
  - **Bolus**: 10-20mg every 10 mins (max 200mg).  
  - **Infusion**: 300mg in 60ml, start at 4ml/hr, double every 30 mins (max 32ml/hr).  
- **Fluid restriction**, catheterize for strict input/output monitoring.  

**Fetal & Delivery Considerations:**  
- Assess fetus **after seizures stop**.  
- **Plan delivery** once maternal condition stabilizes.  

**Key Points:**  
- **MgSO₄ is first-line** for seizure prevention/treatment.  
- **Labetalol** for severe hypertension.  
- **Avoid fluid overload**—restrict IV fluids.  
- **Delivery is definitive treatment**—timing depends on maternal stability.
