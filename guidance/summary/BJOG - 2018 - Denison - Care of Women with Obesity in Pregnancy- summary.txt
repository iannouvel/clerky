The clinical guideline on the care of women with obesity in pregnancy emphasizes the importance of weight optimization before conception and between pregnancies for women with a BMI of 30 kg/m2 or greater. It provides recommendations for antenatal care, fetal monitoring, intrapartum care, and postnatal management tailored to the specific needs and risks associated with maternal obesity. The guideline includes advice on folic acid supplementation, vitamin D deficiency, mental health screening, gestational diabetes, and hypertensive complications. It also addresses issues related to bariatric surgery, breastfeeding, postpartum weight loss, and contraception. Overall, the guideline aims to improve outcomes for pregnant women with obesity and their babies.
