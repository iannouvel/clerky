The clinical guideline on Cervical Cerclage provides recommendations for the insertion of cerclage in women at risk of preterm birth and second trimester fetal loss. It outlines key recommendations for history-indicated and ultrasound-indicated cerclage, as well as emergency cerclage in specific situations. The guideline emphasizes the importance of individualized care and informed decision-making, highlighting potential complications and contraindications to cerclage insertion. It also discusses the types of cerclage techniques, suture materials, and adjuvant management strategies. The guideline calls for further research in certain areas to improve understanding and outcomes related to cerclage procedures.
