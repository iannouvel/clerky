The clinical guideline GP002 focuses on vomiting in pregnancy and hyperemesis gravidarum, providing key principles, responsibilities, baseline assessment, primary care management, care management, inpatient management, and a hyperemesis gravidarum management algorithm. The guideline emphasizes the importance of close assessment, appropriate medications, and emotional support for pregnant women experiencing nausea and vomiting. It highlights the safety of all anti-emetics in pregnancy and outlines a step-wise approach to managing hyperemesis gravidarum, including discharge with thiamine, folic acid, anti-emetics, oral steroids, and follow-up appointments. The guideline aims to ensure effective and comprehensive care for pregnant women with vomiting and hyperemesis gravidarum.
