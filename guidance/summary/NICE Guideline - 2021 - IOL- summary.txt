The NICE guideline on inducing labor provides recommendations for healthcare professionals, commissioners, and pregnant women on the circumstances, methods, assessment, monitoring, pain relief, and managing complications related to labor induction. It emphasizes the importance of informed decision-making, individual preferences, and respectful care. The guideline covers specific scenarios such as prolonged pregnancy, preterm prelabour rupture of membranes, and intrauterine fetal death, offering detailed recommendations for each situation. It discusses various methods of induction, monitoring fetal well-being, and managing potential complications like hyperstimulation. The guideline aims to improve advice and care for pregnant women considering or undergoing induction of labor.
