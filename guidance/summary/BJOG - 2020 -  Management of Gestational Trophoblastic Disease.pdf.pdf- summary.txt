The clinical guideline on the Management of Gestational Trophoblastic Disease provides comprehensive information on the presentation, diagnosis, management, treatment, and follow-up of gestational trophoblastic disease (GTD) and gestational trophoblastic neoplasia (GTN). It includes recommendations on the removal of molar pregnancies, diagnosis of GTN, treatment options, follow-up protocols, contraception, and support for women and their families. The guideline emphasizes the importance of histological examination for accurate diagnosis and the need for specialized care at GTD centers. It also highlights the excellent long-term outcomes for women treated for GTN and provides recommendations for future research in this field.
