The clinical guideline on Pregnancy and Breast Cancer provides comprehensive guidance for healthcare professionals caring for women of childbearing age with a diagnosis or history of breast cancer. It emphasizes multidisciplinary treatment according to UK guidelines, with considerations for pregnancy and breastfeeding. The document discusses optimal management of breast cancer during pregnancy, timing of delivery, contraceptive choices, and advice for women planning pregnancy after breast cancer. It also addresses fertility preservation options, including assisted reproduction techniques, hormone therapy, and surrogacy. The guideline emphasizes the importance of discussing fertility preservation before starting cancer treatment and providing support for young women facing fertility-related concerns.
