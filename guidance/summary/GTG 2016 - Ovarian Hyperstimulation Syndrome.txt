This guideline provides evidence-based recommendations for gtg 2016 - ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome. It is intended for use by healthcare professionals in clinical practice. Executive summary of recommendations Incidence of ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome (OHSS) What is the reported incidence of OHSS? Clinicians must remain alert to the possibility of OHSS in all women undergoing fertility treatment and women should be counselled accordingly.
