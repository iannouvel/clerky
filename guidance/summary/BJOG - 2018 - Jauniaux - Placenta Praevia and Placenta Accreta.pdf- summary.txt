The clinical guideline provides comprehensive recommendations for the diagnosis and management of placenta praevia and placenta accreta. It emphasizes the importance of antenatal diagnosis, tailored care based on individual risk factors, and timely management to reduce maternal and neonatal morbidity and mortality. The guideline covers risk factors, diagnostic methods, delivery planning, surgical techniques, and postpartum care for women with these conditions. It also highlights the need for multidisciplinary care, informed decision-making, and access to specialized services to optimize outcomes. The guideline serves as a valuable resource for healthcare providers involved in the care of women with placenta praevia and placenta accreta.
