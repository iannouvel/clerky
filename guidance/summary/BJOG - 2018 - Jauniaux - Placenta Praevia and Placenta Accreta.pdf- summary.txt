The clinical guideline focuses on the diagnosis and management of Placenta Praevia and Placenta Accreta. It outlines risk factors, antenatal care, delivery considerations, and surgical techniques for these conditions. Key points include the importance of early diagnosis, tailored antenatal care, and delivery planning in specialized units with blood transfusion services. For women with Placenta Accreta, a multidisciplinary team approach is recommended, and uterus-preserving surgical techniques should be performed by experienced surgeons. The guideline emphasizes the need for further research in this area and provides recommendations for patient care based on current evidence and expert consensus.
