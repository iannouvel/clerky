This clinical guideline focuses on managing diabetes in women planning pregnancy or already pregnant, aiming to improve diagnosis of gestational diabetes and empower women to self-manage blood glucose levels. Key recommendations include preconception care, individualized dietary advice, target blood glucose levels, and medication safety considerations. Insulin treatment, continuous glucose monitoring, ketone testing, and birth timing are also discussed. Risks to women and fetuses with diabetes in pregnancy are highlighted, with recommendations for managing complications. The guideline provides specific recommendations for women with pre-existing diabetes and gestational diabetes, emphasizing the importance of tailored care for optimal outcomes.
