This clinical guideline provides comprehensive recommendations for managing diabetes in women planning pregnancy or already pregnant. It emphasizes the importance of preconception planning and care, including blood glucose control, dietary advice, and the use of medications like metformin and insulin. During pregnancy, monitoring blood glucose levels, fetal growth, and wellbeing is crucial, along with managing complications like gestational diabetes and pre-eclampsia. Intrapartum care, neonatal care, and postnatal care are also addressed, highlighting the need for continuous monitoring and support for women with diabetes and their newborn babies. The guideline aims to reduce the risks associated with diabetes in pregnancy and improve outcomes for both mother and baby.
