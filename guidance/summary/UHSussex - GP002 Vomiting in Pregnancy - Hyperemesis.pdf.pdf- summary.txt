The clinical guideline GP002 focuses on vomiting in pregnancy and hyperemesis gravidarum, providing recommendations for healthcare professionals. Key principles include using professional judgment and not being prescriptive. The guideline covers baseline assessment using PUQE scoring, primary care management with safe anti-emetics, and inpatient management with fluids, anti-emetics, and close monitoring. A management algorithm is included for hyperemesis gravidarum, with steps for baseline assessment and acute management. Discharge recommendations include medications like thiamine and folic acid, as well as follow-up appointments. Severe cases may require TPN or oral steroids, with consultation with specialists as needed.
