This clinical guideline provides comprehensive guidance on investigating, diagnosing, and managing viral illnesses in pregnant women, including exposure to rash illnesses. It covers specific infections such as rubella, parvovirus B19, syphilis, and measles, emphasizing the importance of confirmation for appropriate management. The document includes recommendations for testing at different stages of pregnancy, specialist advice for confirmed infections, and monitoring through ultrasound scanning. It also addresses the risks associated with various viral infections during pregnancy, highlighting the need for close monitoring and appropriate management to prevent complications. The guideline does not cover HIV or herpes simplex virus infections in pregnancy.
