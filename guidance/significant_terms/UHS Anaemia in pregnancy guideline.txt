### **Management & Treatment**  
- **Dietary advice (iron-rich foods)**  
- **Recommended daily iron intake (27 mg)**  
- **Oral iron replacement therapy**  
- **Intravenous (IV) iron therapy**  
  - **Indications:**  
    - Non-compliance  
    - Intolerance of oral iron  
    - Malabsorption  
    - GI diseases  
    - Poor response to oral iron  
  - **Contraindications:**  
    - Anaphylaxis history  
    - Hypersensitivity reactions  
    - First trimester of pregnancy  
    - Acute/chronic bacteraemia  
    - Liver disease  
  - **Preparation:** Ferric Derisomaltose (Pharmacosmos)  
  - **Infusion precautions:**  
    - Avoid out-of-hours administration  
    - Minimize iron staining risk  
    - Monitor for adverse effects
