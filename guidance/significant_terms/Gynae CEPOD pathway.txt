Here are the most clinically significant terms and keywords extracted from the guideline, organized by category for clarity:  

### **Clinical Assessment & Criteria**  
- **SIRS Assessment** (Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome)  
  - Temperature **<36°C or >38°C**  
  - Heart rate **>90 bpm**  
  - Respiratory rate **>20/min**  
  - **Admission to Level 11** (if ≥2 SIRS criteria met)  

### **Working Diagnoses (Gynaecological Conditions)**  
- **Bartholin’s Cyst/Abscess**  
- **Vulva Abscess/Haematoma**  
- **Retained Products of Conception (RPOC)**  
- **Ectopic Pregnancy**  

### **Planned Procedures**  
- **Marsupialisation**  
- **Incision & Drainage (I&D)**  
- **Evacuation of Retained Products of Conception (ERPC)**  
- **Diagnostic Laparoscopy**  

### **Checklist & Administrative Steps**  
- **Acute Gynaecological Proforma**  
- **Consent form**  
- **Cremation form** (if applicable)  
- **CEPOD booking form** (CEPOD = **Emergency Operating Pathway**)  
- **CEPOD Coordinator** (Bleep **8061**)  
- **Fast-track CEPOD days**: **Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday PM**  
- **Nurse-led discharge permission** (Yes/No)  

### **Post-Procedure/Admission Steps**  
- **Patient information leaflet**  
- **GAU nurse** (Ext. **4013/4022**)  
- **Day Surgery Unit admission** (Ext. **7242**)  

These terms are critical for clinicians to understand the **assessment criteria, diagnoses, procedures, and administrative workflow** in this gynaecological emergency pathway.
