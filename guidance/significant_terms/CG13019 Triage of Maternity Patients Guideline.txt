Here are the most clinically significant terms and keywords extracted from the guideline, organized by relevance:

### **Core Concepts & Systems**  
- **Triage** (Maternity Triage, Antenatal Triage)  
- **BSOTS system** (standardized assessment, prioritization)  
- **MEOWS** (Modified Early Obstetric Warning Score)  
- **Urgency categories**: **Red/Orange/Yellow/Green**  
- **SBAR tool** (structured handover)  
- **Antenatal Triage Assessment Card (TAC card)**  
- **Symptom-specific algorithms**  

### **Clinical Assessments & Protocols**  
- **Initial assessment within 15 minutes**  
- **Vital signs**: Temperature, Pulse, Respirations, Blood Pressure, Pain levels  
- **Fetal assessment**: Lie, Presentation, Fetal movements, Fetal heart rate  
- **Urinalysis**  
- **VTE Risk Assessment** (thromboprophylaxis for COVID+ patients)  
- **Red flag symptoms**:  
  - O2 saturations **<93%**  
  - **Blue lips**, **audible wheeze**, **chest pain**  
  - **Unable to complete a sentence at rest**  
- **Growth scan & ANC follow-up**  

### **Key Processes & Responsibilities**  
- **Telephone triage** (BSOTS standard form, referral criteria)  
- **Escalation pathway** (Labour Ward Co-ordinator, MDT decision)  
- **Handover** (direct communication, in-person/telephone)  
- **Antenatal Day Assessment Unit (ADAU)** (documentation in MIS)  
- **Neonatal referrals** (community pathway)  

### **High-Risk Conditions & Interventions**  
- **APH** (Antepartum Hemorrhage)  
- **SROM** (Spontaneous Rupture of Membranes)  
- **IOL** (Induction of Labour)  
- **COVID-19 management**:  
  - **Thromboprophylaxis** (10 days post-discharge)  
  - **Hydration & mobility** advice  
  - **High temperature (>37.8°C)** → **call 111/A&E**  

### **Staff & Workflow Requirements**  
- **Midwife/Obstetrician roles** (BSOTS-trained)  
- **Staffing adjustments**
