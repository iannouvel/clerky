### **Eligibility & Contraindications**  
- **Infants at increased TB risk**  
- **Exclusions for BCG vaccination:**  
  - High fever  
  - Cancer/immunosuppressive treatment  
  - HIV-positive status  
  - Generalized skin condition  
  - Passive antibodies received  
  - Steroid treatment  
  - No NBBS (Newborn Blood Spot Screening) result  
  - SCID detected  
  - Immunosuppression exposure in pregnancy/breastfeeding  
- **HIV-unexposed infants (low-risk)**  

### **Vaccine Administration Details**  
- **BCG vaccine dose:**  
  - **Under 12 months:** 0.05ml (intradermal)  
  - **Over 12 months:** 0.1ml (intradermal)  
- **Injection site:** Lateral aspect of left upper arm (deltoid insertion)  
- **Intradermal injection (strictly)**  
- **Concurrent vaccination with inactivated/live vaccines allowed**  
- **No other vaccinations in same arm for 3 months (risk of lymphadenitis)**  

### **Post-Vaccination Care & Monitoring**  
- **Expected reactions:** Sore area, fluid/blood leakage, lump formation  
- **No creams/lotions on injection site**
