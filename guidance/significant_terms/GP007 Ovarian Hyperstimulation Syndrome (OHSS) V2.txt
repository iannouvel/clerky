- **Iatrogenic condition**  
- **Fertility treatment**  
- **Vasoactive products**  
- **Systemic disease**  

### **Diagnosis & Assessment**  
- **Ovarian stimulation history**  
- **Abdominal distension**  
- **Abdominal pain**  
- **Nausea & vomiting**  
- **Differential diagnosis** (ovarian cyst, pelvic infection, intra-abdominal hemorrhage, ectopic pregnancy, appendicitis)  
- **Severity classification** (mild, moderate, severe, critical)

- **Inpatient management** (severe/critical cases)  
- **IV rehydration therapy**  
- **Indwelling catheter** (fluid balance)
