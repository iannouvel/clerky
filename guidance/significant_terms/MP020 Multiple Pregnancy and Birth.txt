Here are the most clinically significant terms and keywords extracted from the guideline, organized by relevance and category:  

### **General Key Terms**  
- **Multiple pregnancy** (twins, triplets, higher-order pregnancies)  
- **Chorionicity** (DCDA, MCDA, MCMA twins)  
- **Amnionicity**  
- **Perinatal morbidity and mortality**  
- **Maternal risks**  
- **Monochorionic pregnancies**  
- **Consultant-led care**  
- **Fetal medicine team**  

### **Diagnostic & Monitoring Terms**  
- **Ultrasound examinations** (dating scan, anomaly scan, growth scans)  
- **Fetal assessment scans**  
- **Chorionicity determination**  
- **Continuous cardiotocography (CTG)**  
- **Fetal scalp electrode**  
- **Portable ultrasound scan (intrapartum)**  

### **Management & Interventions**  
- **Active management of third stage (AMTS)** (oxytocin 10 IU IM)  
- **Physiological management (not recommended in twins/triplets)**  
- **Additional uterotonics (if PPH risk factors)**  
- **Antenatal steroids (for fetal lung maturation)**  
- **External cephalic version (ECV)**  
- **Internal podalic version**  
- **Augmentation of labour (oxytocin)**  
- **Elective LSCS (caesarean section)**  
- **Induction of labour (IOL)**  
- **Epidural use**  

### **Intrapartum & Delivery Terms**  
- **Continuous CTG monitoring (>26 weeks)**  
- **Twin delivery protocols**  
- **Delivery room preparation** (resuscitaires, ultrasound, oxytocin infusion)  
- **Optimum cord clamping**  
- **Interval between twin deliveries (<45 minutes recommended)**  
- **Vaginal birth vs. caesarean section**  
- **Senior obstetrician presence**  
- **Neonatal support at delivery**  

### **Risk Factors & Complications**  
- **Preterm labour**  
- **Postpartum haemorrhage (PPH)**  
- **Twin-twin transfusion syndrome (TTTS)**  
- **Cord entanglement (MCMA twins)**  
- **Fetal compromise (suspicious/pathological CTG)**  
- **Gestational diabetes screening**
