Here are the most clinically significant terms and keywords extracted from the **Cord Prolapse Management** guideline:

### **Clinical Definition & Risk Factors**  
- **Cord prolapse**  
- **Umbilical cord descent**  
- **Ruptured membranes**  
- **Incidence (0.1%–0.6%)**  
- **Risk factors**:  
  - **Long cord**  
  - **Breech presentation**  
  - **Artificial rupture of membranes (ARM)**  
  - **Balloon induction of labor**  

### **Management & Interventions**  
- **Antenatal care for high-risk women**  
- **Obstetric Consultant/Registrar review**  
- **Immediate action upon diagnosis**  
- **Summon help**  
- **Emergency caesarean section (CS)**  
- **Debriefing (postnatal)**  
- **Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) prevention**  

### **Training & Governance**  
- **Obstetric emergency training (annual)**  
- **Clinical incident reporting**  
- **Audit (hospital & community settings)**  
- **NICE (2023) Intrapartum Care standards**
