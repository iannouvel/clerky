Here are the most clinically significant terms and keywords extracted from the guideline, critical for understanding the guidance on **Uterine Artery Embolisation (UAE)**:

### **Indications for UAE:**  
- **Symptomatic fibroids**  
- **Lifestyle-altering symptoms**  
- **Heavy menstrual bleeding**  
- **Severe dysmenorrhoea**  
- **Anaemia**  
- **Fibroid-related pain**  
- **Mass effect (bladder/bowel compression)**  

### **Contraindications:**  
- **Viable pregnancy**  
- **Gynaecological malignancy**  
- **Active uterine infection**  

### **Pre-Procedure Requirements:**  
- **Gynaecological review**  
- **Diagnosis confirmation**  
- **Discussion of alternative treatments**  

### **Procedure Details:**  
- **Embolisation of uterine arteries**  
- **Arterial closure devices**  

### **Post-Procedure Care:**  
- **Complication monitoring**  
- **Pain management**  
- **Follow-up MRI**  
- **Prompt addressing of concerns**  

### **Outcomes:**  
- **High technical success rate**  
- **Satisfactory clinical outcomes**  

These terms encapsulate the essential clinical, procedural, and management aspects of UAE for fibroids.
