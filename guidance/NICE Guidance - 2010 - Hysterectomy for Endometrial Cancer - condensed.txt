Laparoscopic hysterectomy for endometrial cancer is safe and effective, with patient selection and advanced skills required. The procedure aims to provide a shorter hospital stay and recovery period compared to open surgery. Efficacy outcomes include high overall and disease-free survival rates. Safety outcomes show lower rates of postoperative complications with laparoscopic hysterectomy. Further information is available on the NICE website. It is important for healthcare professionals to consider this guidance when making clinical decisions. Local commissioners and providers are responsible for implementing the guidance.
