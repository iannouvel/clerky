Amniocentesis & CVS

Summary

Amniocentesis Summary

  • Perform after 15+0 (earlier leads to higher loss rate, more talipes, and respiratory morbidity)
  • 1% risk of miscarriage (total loss rate including other causes = 1.9%)
  • 3rd trimester amnio safe, higher rate of multiple attempts and blood-stained fluid
  • Sensitising event - needs anti-D if RhD -ve
  • Perform under US guidance
  • Avoid placenta

CVS Summary

  • Perform after 10+0
  • CVS has higher miscarriage rate than amniocentesis
  • If multiple pregnancy, CVS/amniocentesis should be performed by specialist who can also perform selective termination
