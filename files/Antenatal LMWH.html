Antenatal LMWH

Please prescribe on EPMA, on MATERNITY ADMIN. The midwife will then dispense as a TTO and sign on EPMA.

If it's a telephone call, the patient will need to come into ANC to be shown and given a sharps box. Please ask the MCA/Midwife to phone them with a suitable time for both to attend.

For follow-up Enoxaparin, write to GP for repeat or give FP10.

If the patient knows how to use the medication and can access a sharps box, they can then have an FP10, which means they won't have to travel into Sussex House and can use a community pharmacy.

Please avoid using the TTO packs for repeat enoxaparin; repeats can be given on FP10.
