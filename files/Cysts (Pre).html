Risk Assessment
23
Use RMI I (U x M x Ca125)
24
Count 1 for each of the following, given an 'ultrasound score': 1 for each: multilocular cysts, solid areas, metastases, ascites and bilateral lesions.
25
U = 0 (for an ultrasound score of 0), U = 1 (for an ultrasound score of 1), U = 3 (for an ultrasound score of 2–5).
26
M = menopausal status (1 if pre, 3 if post (post = 1 year amenorrhoea or >50 and hysterectomy)
27
28
Consider IOTA rules
29
B rules = Unilocular cysts / Presence of solid components where the largest solid component <7 mm / Presence of acoustic shadowing / Smooth multilocular tumour with a largest diameter <100 mm / No blood flow
30
M rules = Irregular solid tumour / Ascites / At least four papillary structures / Irregular multilocular solid tumour with largest diameter ≥100 mm / Very strong blood flow
