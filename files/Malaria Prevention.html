Malaria infection in pregnancy carries significant risks to mother (anaemia, cerebral infeciton, death) and baby (incluidng low birth weight, stillbirth, miscarriage, preterm birth)

6
Advice
7
Avoid travelling to malaria-endemic regions while pregnant
8
If such travel required, expert advice prior regarding reducing risk of infection.
9
If any fever within 1 year of travel, consider malaria as cause.
10
Prevention measures to consider: repellent spray, knock-down spary, mosquito nets, insecticide treated beds, appropriate clothing.
11
Chemoprophylaxis is usually mefloquine (in 2nd and 3rd trimesters), other options are malarone, doxycyline, chloroquine and proguanil.
