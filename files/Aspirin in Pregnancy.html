Aspirin in Pregnancy

Source: NICE CG62

Indications

  • High risk of pre-eclampsia
  • Pre-existing hypertension
  • Previous severe pre-eclampsia
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Autoimmune disease
  • Diabetes

Dose

  • 75-150 mg daily

When to Start

  • 12-16 weeks gestation

When to Stop

  • 36 weeks gestation
