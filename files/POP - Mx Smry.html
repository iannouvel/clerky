Lifestyle modification: if BMI > 30 advise wt loss / avoid heavy lifting / avoid constipation
Topical oestrogen: if signs of atrophy (consider ring if cognitive impairment)

Surgical options: native tissue repair of anterior and posterior compartments, apical repair can be done by VH +/- culdoplasty, VH +/- SSF, sacrospinous hysteropexy, colpocleisis, Manchester repair
