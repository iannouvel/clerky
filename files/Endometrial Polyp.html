Background and Aetiology
Aetiology unknown Polyps show loss of apoptotic regulation and over expression of oestrogen and progesterone receptors Polyps can increase risk of miscarriage and infertility. Hysteroscopic polypectomy has been shown to improve the rate of spontaneous conception regardless of size and number of polyps.
