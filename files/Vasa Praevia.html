Key Points
4
Refers to unprotected fetal vessels running below presenting part of fetus over the cervical os.
5
Incidence is 1 in 2000-6000 pregnancies
6
Typically presents at time of membrane rupture with fresh PV bleeding and fetal heart abnormalities. Fetal mortality is around 60%
7
If diagnosed antenatally (usually on anomaly scan), advise confirm in 3rd trimester, prophylactic steroids at 32 weeks and delivery by C-section between 34 and 36 weeks.
