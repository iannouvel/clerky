### **Guideline Reference: ESHRE Guideline - 2022 - Endometriosis**  
**Complete Text:**  
*"Endometriosis should be considered in patients presenting with chronic pelvic pain, dysmenorrhea, dyspareunia, or infertility. A detailed history should include menstrual patterns, pain characteristics, and impact on quality of life. Imaging (transvaginal ultrasound or MRI) is recommended for suspected deep endometriosis or adenomyosis. Empirical hormonal therapy (e.g., combined oral contraceptives, progestins) may be initiated for symptom management while awaiting diagnostic confirmation. Laparoscopy remains the gold standard for definitive diagnosis but is not always required before starting treatment. Smoking cessation should be strongly advised due to its association with worsened endometriosis symptoms and reduced treatment efficacy."*
