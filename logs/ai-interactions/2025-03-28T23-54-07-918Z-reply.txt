### **Guideline Text (BJOG - 2018 - Denison - Care of Women with Obesity in Pregnancy)**  
**Key Recommendations:**  
1. **Preconception and antenatal care:**  
   - Women with BMI ≥30 should receive pre-pregnancy counselling on risks (e.g., miscarriage, gestational diabetes, thromboembolism).  
   - Early referral to specialist obstetric care for BMI ≥40.  
   - Offer early OGTT (at booking, repeat at 24–28 weeks if normal).  
   - Thromboprophylaxis assessment for BMI ≥40 or additional risk factors.  

2. **Weight management:**  
   - Dietetic referral for individualized advice.  
   - Avoid weight loss during pregnancy; focus on healthy eating and physical activity.  

3. **Postnatal care:**  
   - Long-acting reversible contraception (LARC) strongly advised.  
   - Postnatal weight management support.  

4. **Monitoring:**  
   - Regular Hb checks for anaemia.  
   - Close glucose monitoring in women with prior gestational diabetes.
