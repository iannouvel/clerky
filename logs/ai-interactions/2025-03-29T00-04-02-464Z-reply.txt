### **Guideline Reference:**  
**BJOG - 2018 - Denison - Care of Women with Obesity in Pregnancy**  
*Key Recommendations:*  
- Women with a BMI ≥40 should be referred to a specialist antenatal team for multidisciplinary care.  
- Early screening for gestational diabetes (OGTT at first antenatal visit if BMI ≥30) is recommended.  
- Prophylactic LMWH should be considered in women with BMI ≥40, especially with additional risk factors (e.g., prior gestational diabetes).  
- High-dose folic acid (5mg daily) is advised for women with BMI ≥30.  
- Contraception counselling should include long-acting reversible methods (LARCs) due to higher risks of rapid repeat pregnancy.  
- Lifestyle interventions (diet, exercise, smoking cessation) should be offered.
