### **Guideline Referenced:**  
**UHS Anaemia in Pregnancy UHS-CG-0008-2023 (3)**  
*Complete Text:*  
"All pregnant patients with iron-deficiency anemia (Hb < 11 g/dL in 1st/3rd trimester, < 10.5 g/dL in 2nd trimester) should receive oral iron supplementation (ferrous sulfate 200 mg daily) and dietary advice. If Hb does not improve within 2 weeks, intravenous iron therapy should be considered. Severe anemia (Hb < 7 g/dL) warrants urgent hematology referral. Fatigue and pallor should prompt immediate Hb assessment."
