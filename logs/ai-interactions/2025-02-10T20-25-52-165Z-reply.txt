Given your symptoms, we may need to consider further evaluation, including a pelvic ultrasound to assess the endometrial thickness and rule out any underlying issues, such as endometrial hyperplasia or malignancy.

**Patient:** Malignancy? That sounds scary. What are the chances of that?

**Clinician:** It's essential to investigate further to determine the cause of the thickening. Most cases of endometrial thickening are benign, especially if the thickness is below a certain threshold. However, we need to be thorough in our evaluation to ensure your health and well-being.
