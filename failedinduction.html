Discussion: Failed Induction of Labour and options

  • Further induction with Propess or Prostin:

    • Risks: Increased risk of uterine hyperstimulation, possible fetal distress.

    Benefits: Can lead to a successful vaginal delivery, avoiding surgical intervention.

  • Rest day:

    • Risks: Delay in delivery may increase risk of complications, particularly if there are existing maternal or fetal concerns.

    Benefits: Provides time for the cervix to ripen naturally, potentially leading to a more successful subsequent induction.

  • Caesarean section:

    • Risks: Surgical risks including infection, bleeding, and longer recovery period.

    Benefits: Immediate delivery which can be beneficial in certain medical situations.
