Discussion: Failed Induction of Labour and options

  • Options:
      • Further induction with Propess or Prostin - risks: increased risk of uterine hyperstimulation, possible fetal distress, benefits: can lead to a successful vaginal delivery, avoiding surgical intervention.
    1. Rest day: risks: Delay in delivery may increase risk of complications, particularly if there are existing maternal or fetal concerns; benefits: provides time for the cervix to ripen naturally, potentially leading to a more successful subsequent induction.
    2. Caesarean section. Risks: surgical risks including infection, bleeding, and longer recovery period; benefits: urgent (usually Cat 3) delivery which can be beneficial in certain medical situations.
