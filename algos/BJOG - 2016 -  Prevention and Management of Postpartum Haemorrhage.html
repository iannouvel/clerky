Monitoring and Documentation

Accurate documentation of a delivery with PPH is essential. Inadequate documentation can lead to medicolegal consequences.

Record staff, events, pharmacological agents, surgical intervention, mother's condition, and fluid/blood products given.

Offer debriefing to women after obstetric hemorrhage to address psychological effects. Discuss future pregnancy and arrange appropriate investigations.
