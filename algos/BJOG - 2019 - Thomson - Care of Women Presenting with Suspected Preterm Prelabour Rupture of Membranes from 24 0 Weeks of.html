```html PPROM Clinical Guidelines

Variables

  • Maternal history
  • Sterile speculum examination
  • IGFBP-1/PAMG-1 test
  • Erythromycin administration
  • Corticosteroids administration
  • Chorioamnionitis assessment
  • Magnesium sulfate administration
  • Expectant management

Guidance

Diagnosis of PPROM: Based on maternal history followed by a sterile speculum examination. Consider IGFBP-1 or PAMG-1 test if no amniotic fluid is observed.

Antibiotic Treatment: Administer erythromycin for 10 days or until the woman is in established labor.

Corticosteroids: Offer to women with PPROM between 24+0 and 33+6 weeks' gestation.

Chorioamnionitis Diagnosis: Use clinical assessment, maternal blood tests, and fetal heart rate monitoring.

Magnesium Sulfate: Offer to women in established labor with PPROM between 24+0 and 29+6 weeks for neuroprotection.

Expectant Management: Continue until 37+0 weeks for women with PPROM after 24+0 weeks' gestation.

```
