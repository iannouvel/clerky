Guidance:

If weeks of gestation is less than 16 and other causes ruled out, it is NVP.

If symptoms start early, cause inability to eat/drink normally, and lead to dehydration, it is HG.

If first line antiemetics are ineffective, consider second line options.

If signs of dehydration are present, consider intravenous fluid replacement.
