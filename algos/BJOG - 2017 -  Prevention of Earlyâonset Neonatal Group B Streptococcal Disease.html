Prevention of Early-onset Neonatal Group B Streptococcal Disease

Interactive decision support tool based on clinical guidelines for GBS management

Antenatal Questions

Has the patient had a previous baby affected by GBS disease?
Was GBS bacteriuria detected during this pregnancy?
Has GBS been detected incidentally or through testing?

Intrapartum Questions

Is the patient in labor?
Is this a preterm labor (<37 weeks)?
Is the patient pyrexial (fever ≥38°C)?
Is this a planned caesarean section without labor?
Is the patient a known GBS carrier at term?

Postnatal Questions

Did the mother receive adequate intrapartum antibiotic prophylaxis (IAP)?
Is the baby showing any clinical signs of infection?
Is the baby term (≥37 weeks) without risk factors?

Guidance

Please select your clinical context and answer the questions to receive personalized guidance.

Important: This tool provides guidance based on clinical guidelines but should not replace clinical judgment. Always consider the individual patient's circumstances.

Based on RCOG guidelines for Prevention of Early-onset Neonatal Group B Streptococcal Disease.

This tool is for clinical decision support only and should be used by healthcare professionals.
