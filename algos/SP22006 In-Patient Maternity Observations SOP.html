Baseline Observations

For all women/people admitted to the maternity unit or being seen in DAU/Triage, baseline measurements of vital signs must be recorded on admission.

This includes:

  • Pulse rate
  • Blood pressure
  • Respiratory rate
  • Temperature
  • Conscious level (ACVPU)
  • Urine output
