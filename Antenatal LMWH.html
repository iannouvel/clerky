Antenatal LMWH

Please prescribe on EPMA, on MATERNITY ADMIN. The midwife will then dispense as a TTO and sign on EPMA.

If it's a telephone call- the patient will need to come in to ANC to be shown and given a sharps box. Please ask the MCA/Midwife to phone them with a suitable time for both - to attend.

For follow up Enoxaparin, write to GP for repeat - or give FP10.

If the patient knows how to use the medication - and can access a sharps box, they can then have an FP10- this means they won't have to travel in to Sussex house, and can use a community pharmacy.

Please can we avoid using the TTO packs for repeat enoxaparin, repeats can be given on FP1
