Syntocinon Augmentation: Risks, Benefits, Alternatives

  • Risks are: possible overstimulation of the uterus, which may decrease oxygen supply to the baby, rare risk of uterine rupture, especially in women with a previous cesarean scar, intensified contractions can lead to increased pain and a higher request for pain relief methods, increased chance of postpartum hemorrhage and potential for water intoxication.
  • Benefits are: facilitates the progression of labor by strengthening and regulating contractions, potentially helps in avoiding a cesarean delivery, considered safe when used appropriately under careful monitoring.
  • Alternatives are: await natural labour with delays to delivery leading to prolongation of pregnancy and, if membranes ruptured, increased risk of chorio to both mum and baby.
