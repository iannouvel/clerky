Syntocinon Augmentation: Risks and Benefits

Benefits:

  • Facilitates the progression of labor by strengthening and regulating contractions.
  • Potentially helps in avoiding a cesarean delivery.
  • Considered safe when used appropriately under careful monitoring.

Risks:

  • Possible overstimulation of the uterus, which may decrease oxygen supply to the baby.
  • Rare risk of uterine rupture, especially in women with a previous cesarean scar.
  • Intensified contractions can lead to increased pain and a higher request for pain relief methods.
  • Increased chance of postpartum hemorrhage and potential for water intoxication.

Decisions regarding Syntocinon augmentation should be made after evaluating individual circumstances and weighing the potential benefits against the associated risks.
