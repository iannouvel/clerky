Fetal Heart Rate Categorization

Contractions in 10 minutes

Fewer than 5 contractions

The contractions are categorised as: White

5 or more contractions

The contractions are categorised as: Amber

Baseline Fetal Heart Rate

110 to 160 beats a minute

The fetal heart rate baseline is categorised as: White

100 to 109 beats a minute or 20 bpm increase or unable to determine baseline

The fetal heart rate baseline is categorised as: Amber

Below 100 beats a minute or above 160 beats a minute

The fetal heart rate baseline is categorised as: Red

Variability

5 to 25 beats a minute

The variability is categorised as: White

Fewer than 5 beats a minute for 30-50 minutes, or more than 25 beats a minute for up to 10 minutes

The variability is categorised as: Amber

Fewer than 5 beats a minute for more than 50 minutes, more than 25 beats a minute for more than 10 minutes, or sinusoidal

The variability is categorised as: Red

Decelerations

No decelerations, early decelerations, or non-evolving variable decelerations

The decelerations are categorised as: White

Repetitive variable decelerations with concerning characteristics for less than 30 minutes, or late decelerations for less than 30 minutes

The decelerations are categorised as: Amber

Repetitive variable decelerations with concerning characteristics for more than 30 minutes, late decelerations for more than 30 minutes, acute bradycardia, or prolonged deceleration lasting 3 minutes or more

The decelerations are categorised as: Red
